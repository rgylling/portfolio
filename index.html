About Me

I have enjoyed development and design since the minute I started. Through an intensive software curriculum at Code Fellows in Portland, Oregon, I have perfected my front-end development and design skills. Soon after, I began working as a Web Developer at a Digital Agency in Temecula, Ca. This position inspired me to continue my education at Flatiron School Online and learn Full Stack Development.

Before pursuing Software Development, I worked in the engineering field as a Senior Field Service Technician at AFM Engineering for 4 years, building automation machinery and traveling the country performing complex repairs. After many years serving AFM Engineering I decided to follow my passion and pursue a career in software development. My experience in the engineering field has taught me valuable skills such as writing software for Programmable Logic Controllers, and troubleshooting millisecond critical automation systems which has prepared me to excel in the field of software development.

As a website developer at a Digital Marketing Agency, I had the opportunity to participate in many different projects that ranged from Wordpress Development to Websites built from scratch, Front-end Development, Responsive Design, Search Engine Optimization, Company Branding, Web Applications and more. My ultimate goal is to find a company where I will thrive and continue building incredible software that I will be proud to have created.
