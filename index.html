About me

Hello there and welcome to my page! My names Randy, I am 25 years old and I am from Murrieta California. I enjoy playing guitar, hiking, and pretty much anything outdoors (though programming is starting to hinder that). Currently I am living in Portland Oregon after attending Code Fellows web development bootcamp. How did I go from Murrieta to Portland you ask? Well before I answer that I think its best to tell you a little bit about my background. Before Code Fellows I was an Engineer technician at an engineering firm in California. I basically built automation machinery and traveled around fixing them. I always tried to continuously push myself to learn more which got me into the programming side of the machinery. After learning how to program machinery I started developing a passion for programming which led me to web development. I started off learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript in my spare time until I decided I wanted to do it full time. I eventually quit my job and that is what led me to Code Fellows! The rest is history... Hopefully..
